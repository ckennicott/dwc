Game Overview

Power hour is a simple yet challenging drinking game. The objective is to drink one shot of beer each minute for an hour. Make it to the end and you'll have downed 7-8 beers.

What You'll Need

  • 1 shot glass for each player
  • Lots of beer (7-8 for each player)
  • The power hour timer below

How to Play

Fill a shot glass with beer and start the timer below. Everytime you hear a tone down another shot of beer.
