Grab some friends and play!

Who will get the Kings Cup?

How to play

  • Place an empty cup where everyone can see it. This will be the "Kings Cup"
  • Use the draw button to draw a new card.
  • Follow the instructions associated with the card you drew.
  • If you draw a king you can add drinks to the kings cup.
  • If you draw the 4th king, you must drink the contents of the center cup.
