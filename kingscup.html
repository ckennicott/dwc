Game Overview

Kings cup is a fun social drinking game. Players take turns drawing cards from a deck. Each card has an associated mini game.

What You'll Need

  • Some friends
  • Drinks (1 for each player). Tip: A good mix of drinks (beer, cocktails, wine coolsers) makes the game more intersting.
  • A deck of cards or the virtual deck below

How to Play

Click the "Draw" button below to draw a card. Follow the instructions associated with the card you drew. If you draw a king you can add drinks to the kings cup. If you draw the 4th king, you must drink the contents of the center cup.
